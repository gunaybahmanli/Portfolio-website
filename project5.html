Project 5: Book Sales Analysis

Short description: Analyzing book sales data from 2000-2016 to identify top authors, genres, and revenue trends.

Introduction

This project explores historical book sales data including author ratings, genres, sales volume, and publisher revenue. The aim is to understand market trends and identify top-performing authors and books.

Objective

To provide insights into book sales patterns over time, genre popularity, and publisher revenue contributions.

Data & Tools

  • Dataset: Book sales between 2000-2016 with ratings and revenue data
  • Tools: Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Power BI
  • Excel for preliminary analysis

Methodology

Data cleaning, aggregation by author and genre, and visualization of revenue, sales, and ratings were performed. Dashboards were created to illustrate trends and top-selling books.

Python Scripts

Example Python code to calculate total units sold per author:

import pandas as pd

data = pd.read_csv("book_sales.csv")
author_sales = data.groupby('Author')['units_sold'].sum().sort_values(ascending=False)
print(author_sales.head(10))

Visualizations

Charts illustrate top authors, bestselling books, genre performance, and revenue distribution.

Insights & Conclusion

  • Top authors consistently produce high-selling books across multiple genres.
  • Genre popularity shifts over time, impacting sales strategy.
  • Data-driven insights support publishers in marketing and stock decisions.
