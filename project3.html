Project 3: Customer Purchase Behavior Analysis

Short description: Analyzing customer electronics sales data to extract insights on purchasing patterns, loyalty, and product trends.

Introduction

The project focuses on exploring customer transactions from September 2023 to September 2024. We aim to understand purchase frequency, product popularity, and demographic patterns.

Objective

The objective is to provide actionable insights into customer behavior to help electronics retailers optimize marketing campaigns, stock management, and loyalty programs.

Data & Tools

  • Dataset: Customer electronics sales data (transactions, demographics, payment methods)
  • Tools: Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
  • Excel for summary analysis

Methodology

Data cleaning, feature engineering (age brackets, total sales per customer, loyalty changes) and exploratory data analysis were performed. Trends were visualized to detect patterns and anomalies.

Python Scripts

Example Python code used for analyzing top customers:

import pandas as pd

data = pd.read_csv("customer_sales.csv")
top_customers = data.groupby('Customer_ID')['Total_Price'].sum().sort_values(ascending=False).head(10)
print(top_customers)

Visualizations

Visualizations show top-selling products, customer age distribution, and purchasing trends.

Insights & Conclusion

  • Loyalty members tend to make larger purchases and more frequent transactions.
  • Customers aged 25-40 contribute the most to total sales.
  • Python and Pandas analysis helped identify top 10 customers and most popular products.
